Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025. This policy explains how Grokipedia collects, uses, and protects your information.

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Grokipedia (operating at grokexpedia.us) collects when you use our website, how we use that information, and the choices you have. We've written this in plain language — not just legal boilerplate — because we think you deserve to actually understand it.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you use our contact form or subscribe to our newsletter, you provide us with:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Any message content you choose to submit

We collect only what you voluntarily provide. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to read our content.

Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, we collect certain information automatically when you visit:

  • Log data: Your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and time of visit. This data is used for security, debugging, and understanding how the site is used.
  • Cookies: Small text files stored in your browser. We use session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain for a set period). See the Cookies section below for details.
  • Usage data: Aggregated, anonymized data about how visitors navigate the site — which articles are read, how long pages are viewed, and similar analytics. This data is not linked to individual identities.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for these specific purposes:

  • To respond to your messages: If you contact us, we use your name and email to reply.
  • To send newsletters: If you subscribe, we use your email to send the content you requested. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • To improve the site: Aggregate analytics help us understand which content is most useful, so we can produce more of it.
  • To maintain security: Log data helps us detect and respond to security threats.
  • To serve advertising: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites. See the Advertising section below.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for purposes beyond those listed above.

3. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function. These cannot be disabled without affecting site functionality.
  • Analytics cookies: Used to understand how visitors use the site. We use Google Analytics, which sets cookies to collect aggregate, anonymized usage data.
  • Advertising cookies: Set by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements. These cookies may track your browsing across websites to personalize ads.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on the site. You can opt out of personalized Google advertising at google.com/settings/ads.

4. Google AdSense and Advertising

We participate in the Google AdSense program, which displays advertisements on this site. Google uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your interests and browsing history.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info. For more information on Google's data practices, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Advertisements on this site are clearly labeled. Our editorial content is not influenced by advertisers, and we do not allow advertiser influence over article topics, conclusions, or framing.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense: Advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts: Typography. Fonts are loaded from Google's servers; this may involve Google collecting your IP address. Google Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for up to 12 months for the purpose of responding to follow-up queries. Newsletter subscriber information is retained until you unsubscribe. Server logs are retained for up to 90 days for security purposes. Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: The right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: The right to request that we correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: The right to request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Opt-out: The right to opt out of newsletter communications at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@grokexpedia.us.

8. Children's Privacy

Grokipedia is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the personal information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and we encourage you to use discretion about the personal information you submit through any website.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will provide more prominent notice. Your continued use of the site after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at privacy@grokexpedia.us or use the contact form.
