Our Corrections Policy

We take accuracy seriously. If you believe an article contains a factual error, please tell us: the article title, the specific claim you believe is incorrect, and — if you have it — a reference to a more accurate source. We investigate all correction reports and respond. If the correction is valid, we update the article and acknowledge the change. We don't hide corrections or quietly edit without transparency.
