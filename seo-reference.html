Google AdSense Compliance Checklist

✓ Content Quality
  • 10 long-form articles (1,200–1,800+ words each)
  • No thin content — every article delivers substantial value
  • No duplicate content — all articles are original
  • Human editorial review on all content
  • Articles cover topics thoroughly, not superficially
✓ Site Structure
  • Clear navigation menu on every page
  • About Us page with author details and mission
  • Contact page with working contact form
  • Privacy Policy (AdSense-compliant)
  • Disclaimer and Terms & Conditions
✓ Trust Signals
  • Named authors with credentials and bios
  • Published/updated dates on all articles
  • Editorial standards publicly stated
  • Corrections policy documented
  • E-E-A-T framework implemented throughout
✓ Technical
  • Responsive design — mobile-first layout
  • Semantic HTML5 (header, nav, article, footer)
  • JSON-LD schema on every page
  • Canonical URLs on all pages
  • No misleading claims or deceptive navigation
