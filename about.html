About Grokipedia

An independent knowledge platform built on the belief that understanding — not just information — is what matters.

Our Mission

Grokipedia exists to close the gap between information availability and genuine understanding. The internet has made more information accessible than any previous generation could have imagined — and yet the gap between having access to information and actually understanding it remains wide. Wikipedia can tell you what machine learning is. Grokipedia is built to help you understand it.

We focus on artificial intelligence, technology, and the ideas at their intersection — because these are the subjects most urgently in need of accessible, accurate, jargon-free explanation. AI is shaping decisions that affect billions of people. Those people deserve to understand what's happening, and they deserve that explanation without needing a computer science degree to follow it.

Our Editorial Philosophy

We take our editorial standards seriously, and we hold ourselves to them publicly.

Comprehension over documentation. We don't just document facts — we explain them in ways that build real understanding. An article that a reader cannot follow doesn't serve them, regardless of how accurate it is.

Accuracy without compromise. Accessible doesn't mean imprecise. We write for non-specialists, but we don't oversimplify to the point of being wrong. When a topic is genuinely complex, we say so rather than papering over it with false simplicity.

Neutral perspective. On topics where reasonable people disagree — including many questions in AI ethics and policy — we present multiple perspectives rather than advocating for one. Our job is to inform your thinking, not direct it.

Honest acknowledgment of uncertainty. When knowledge is genuinely uncertain, we say so. When a question doesn't have a settled answer, we say that too. Pretending to certainty we don't have is a form of misinformation.

Prompt correction. When we get something wrong — and all publications do, at some point — we correct it promptly and transparently. Errors are not hidden or quietly edited; they are acknowledged.

How We Create Content

Our editorial process has several stages designed to ensure quality and accuracy.

Topic selection. Articles are selected based on their importance and the gap between how commonly they're encountered and how well they're explained elsewhere. We prioritize topics where the available explanations are inadequate for non-specialist readers.

Research and drafting. Initial drafts draw on the xAI Grok model as a drafting tool, combined with research from primary sources, academic literature, and authoritative secondary sources. AI assistance is used to accelerate drafting, not to replace editorial judgment.

Expert review. Every article is reviewed by our editorial team for accuracy, clarity, completeness, and appropriate nuance. Review focuses particularly on technical claims, which are verified against primary sources.

Editorial polish. Final editing ensures consistent voice, appropriate depth, clear organization, and readability for the intended audience. We also ensure that articles connect appropriately to related content on the platform.

Ongoing updates. AI and technology move quickly. Articles are reviewed and updated when significant developments change our understanding of a topic. Update dates are noted on articles that have been revised.

E-E-A-T Commitment

We take Google's E-E-A-T framework (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) seriously — not because of SEO implications, but because it codifies exactly what we believe a knowledge platform should be. Our authors have genuine expertise in their subject areas. Our editorial standards are rigorous. Our commitment to accuracy and honest acknowledgment of uncertainty reflects a genuine commitment to trust.

We don't inflate our expertise or make credentials we don't have. The team is small, the coverage is focused, and the quality standard is consistent.

Independence and Funding

Grokipedia is an independent publication. Our editorial decisions are not influenced by advertisers, sponsors, or any commercial relationship. Where advertising appears on the site, it is clearly labeled and does not influence article selection, framing, or conclusions.

We cover xAI and Grok extensively because they're the most relevant topic for our platform's focus — not because we have a commercial relationship with xAI. Our coverage of Grok's limitations, and our fair comparisons with competitor products, reflect our editorial independence.
